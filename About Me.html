I'm Ellie

Hi, my name is Ellie, I’m local to West Malling. I decided 2025 was the year for me to take the plunge and open my own home help business. I have a strong passion for helping and inspiring others. I promise to do everything I can and work to the best of my ability to make sure you get the most out of my service. I would describe myself as someone with a big heart, always wanting to bring joy and laughter to someone's life. I pride myself in being very family oriented, my nana is incredibly special to me, she is an incredibly strong and resilient woman, playing a massive part in me setting up this business. I’ve had just under two years of experience working with vulnerable adults of all ages. Throughout the two years I have worked with adults with dementia, long covid and mental health issues such as hoarding, social anxiety and depression. I’m here to make your day to day life easier.
